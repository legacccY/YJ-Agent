\caption{\textbf{The reliability\,$\times$\,recoverability decision surface.}
Per-dimension diagnostic AUC over a $5\times5$ degradation\,$\times$\,severity grid
($n=360$/cell). \emph{Reliability} = AUC drop from the mildest (S1) to the most severe
(S5) level. \emph{Recoverability} = $\Delta_{\text{rec}}=\mathrm{AUC}_{\text{enh}}-\mathrm{AUC}$
with a bootstrap $2000$-sample $95\%$ CI; a regime is significant only where the CI
excludes zero. Exactly one of all $25$ cells significantly \emph{hurts} (contrast at S5,
$\alpha{=}0.19$), reported transparently; completeness stays statistically inconclusive
(CI straddles zero, hence \emph{partially recoverable}, not irreversible). Full per-cell
values in \cref{app:surface}.}
